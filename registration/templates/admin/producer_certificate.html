Annexure-I
Terms and Conditions
  1. Producers are obligated to meet their Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) requirements by purchasing EPR certificates, proportionate to their obligations, from Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facilities (RVSFs) only.
  2. A Producer can purchase Extended Producer Responsibility certificate limited to its Extended Producer Responsibility obligation of current year plus any leftover obligation of preceding years.
  3. An Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) certificate purchased by a Producer shall be automatically adjusted against their obligations under these rules, with priority given to fulfilling earlier or preceding years' obligations.
  4. An Extended Producer Responsibility certificate used by the producer to meet its Extended Producer Responsibility obligations shall not be exchanged again.
  5. An Extended Producer Responsibility certificate purchased by the producer is not transferrable to any other producer.
  6. A Producer may carry forward up to 30% of their Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) targets from any given year to the subsequent four years for the EPR target compliance.
  7. The EPR certificates shall be subject to audit by Central Pollution Control Board or any other agencies authorized in this regard.
  8. If the producer submits false information or wilfully conceals information for registration or return or report or information required to be provided or furnished under Environment Protection (End-of-Life Vehicles) Rules, 2025, or in case of any irregularity, the registration of such entity may be revoked by the Central Pollution Control Board after giving an opportunity to be heard and in addition, environmental compensation (EC) charges may also be levied as per rule 15 of Environment Protection (End-of-Life Vehicles) Rules, 2025.
  9. The producer shall be responsible to file annual returns before 30th June following the financial year to which the return relates.
  10. All the transactions w.r.t purchase of EPR certificate shall be recorded and submitted by the producers on the portal at the time of filing annual returns.
  11. The provision of EC as stipulated under rule 15 of Environment Protection (End-of-Life Vehicles) Rules, 2025 shall be applicable in case of any violation/non- compliance.
  12. The Board reserves right to cancel/amend/revoke the registration at any time as per the policy of the Board or Government.
  13. In case of any violation of the above condition, your registration will be suspended/ cancelled.
